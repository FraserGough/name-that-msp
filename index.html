How to play

This is a flash card game for learning the faces of current members of the Scottish Parliament, their parties and the electoral area they have been elected to represent.

It's simple. You'll be shown a card that has a photo of a member, and blanked out under that the member's name, party and electoral area. Click the blanks to reveal the information.

If you were right about the information, press check to move on to the next card.

If you were wrong about the information, or couldn't even hazard a guess, press close, the fails count will increase by 1 and the card will be put to the back of the deck so you can try again.

The aim is to get through all of the cards with as few fails as possible.
